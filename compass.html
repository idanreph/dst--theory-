System Diagnostic

Answer all 15 questions about your system. Be specific — vague answers produce vague diagnosis. The more real you are, the sharper the output.

Q1 · System Boundary
What is the system?
Define exactly what is being analyzed. If the boundary is vague, the diagnosis will be vague.
Q2 · Failure Definition
What does failure actually mean?
Concrete, real-world terms only. Not "things get worse" — what specifically breaks?
Q3 · Regime
What regime is the system in?
Elastic: recovers through real restoration. Plastic: appears stable via masking. Residual: delayed failure, time is the main variable.
Q4 · Θ — True Tolerance
How much real buffer remains?
Not "does it work today?" — how much genuine capacity to absorb stress before regime change?
Q5 · D — Irreversible Damage
What damage is accumulating underneath?
Even when everything still seems to work. Slow, structural, hard to reverse.
Q6 · σ — Stress Engine
What is the self-reinforcing killer loop?
Not the initial pressure — the feedback mechanism that makes stress nonlinear and self-amplifying.
Q7 · ρ — True Restoration
What actually rebuilds future tolerance?
Not appearance. Not symptom suppression. What genuinely increases Θ?
Q8 · κ — Displacement Channel
Where is stress being hidden or exported?
If you cannot name κ, you do not yet understand why the system still appears alive.
Q9 · κmax — Sink Saturation
When does the displacement channel run out?
The point where delay stops scaling. The moment the masking stops working.
Q10 · Lying Metric
Which metric looks healthy while the system worsens?
The false reassurance signal. The number everyone watches that tells the wrong story.
Q11 · Earliest Θ Proxy
What is the earliest real warning signal?
Moves before conventional indicators. The canary before the collapse.
Q12 · Earliest κ-Saturation Proxy
What is the first sign displacement is failing?
When does the masking start showing cracks before it breaks completely?
Q13 · Transition Boundary
What flips the system into a worse regime?
Q13.5 · κ-Cap Check — Critical
What displacement must be capped before the rail works?
⚠ Building restoration while displacement is fully active is usually ineffective. The system routes back into masking. Name what must be reduced first.
Q14 · Correct Rail
What is the right intervention?
Buffer · Export · Pulse · Reset · Truth — most bad strategies are "more κ" pretending to be a rail.
Q15 · Payer & Sink Map
Who pays when this breaks, and who is absorbing it now?
